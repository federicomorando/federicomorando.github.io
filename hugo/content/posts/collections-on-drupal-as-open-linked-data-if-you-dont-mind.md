I'm thinking about a technical solution to easily put online collections of ancient coins, possibly using an open linked data approach...  
At the moment, I'm starting to look into the following Drupal modules (evolving list):

*   [Content Construction Kit (CCK)](http://drupal.org/project/cck) and its 
    [Content Construction Kit Handbook](http://drupal.org/node/101723);
*   [FileField CKK](http://drupal.org/project/filefield);
*   [Linked Data](http://drupal.org/project/linked_data).  

Actually, my favourite solution would likely go through CKAN, but I doubt that my cheap Aruba hosting can support it at the moment...
